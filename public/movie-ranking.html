The Best Movies of All Time According to Cotton-Eyed Joe

My top 3 movies of all time.

Dune 2

This is my favorite movie bar none. Awesome action scenes, immersive storytelling, imaginative sci-fi elements, and incredible visuals all reasons why this is my favorite film.

The Lord of the Rings, The Return of the King

This was my favorite movie for a long time before I saw Dune 2. It is my second movie for a lot of the same reasons why Dune 2 is now my favorite.

Inception

I really enjoy the mind-bending sci-fi elements of this film, and it also has great action scenes too.
